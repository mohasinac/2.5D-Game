## PLASTIC GEN — ATTACK RINGS

Attack Rings are the topmost layer. They set the primary attack style (Smash, Upper, Defense, Survival) and recoil profile. Two-piece ARs have a Sub-Attack Ring (SAR). All standard Plastic Gen ARs are **ABS plastic** unless otherwise noted. ARs mount to the Weight Disk, which is compatible with all standard Blade Bases.

**Spin compatibility key** — "Universal": shape is symmetric; both spin directions are viable. "R preferred" / "L preferred": asymmetric slope gives better contact on that spin. "EG Base": designed for Engine Gear base only.

# HARD METAL SYSTEM (HMS) — PARTS

HMS beyblades use all-metal frames with rubber-ringed Running Cores. Parts are **not interchangeable** with Plastic Gen. All HMS ARs are **die-cast zinc alloy**; CWDs are ABS plastic or metal as noted; RCs are ABS plastic with tip materials varying by type.

## HMS — RUNNING CORES

Running Cores (RCs) are the bottom module housing the spinning tip. Interchangeable between all HMS beys. Bodies are **ABS plastic**. Tip materials and shapes vary significantly. Three mold variants affect body durability — the tip itself is unaffected by mold number.

### Mold History
| Mold | Found In | Body Notes |
|------|----------|-----------|
| Mold 1 | Earliest releases up to Wolborg MS | Thin soft edge; weakest; prong shifts upward during play |
| Mold 2 | Phantom Fox MS onward (MA-15+) | Slightly thicker, defined edge; stronger; standard competitive choice |
| Mold 3 | Hasbro early releases only | Oversized prong; most durable; rarest |

## HMS — BIT PROTECTORS (BPs)

Bit Protectors are the small cap at the top center of the HMS bey (equivalent to Face Chip in Plastic Gen). **Material: ABS plastic** (standard) or **metal** (special RBA releases — negligible weight difference). BPs do not affect gameplay.

- **Small Type BP** — narrower; works best with Mold 1 + 2 RC prong geometry
- **Large Type BP** — wider; fits Mold 3 RC prong
- Metal BPs (RBA series): die-cast alloy; same geometry as Large Type

## PLASTIC GEN — COMPATIBILITY NOTES

**Spin Gear compatibility:**
- Standard (Ten/Eight/Six) WDs and Wide WDs (WD/WA/WS) are universal across all Plastic Gen blade bases.
- Standard Spin Gears (SG/Neo SG) mount in all non-EG blade bases.
- Engine Gear bases require Engine Gear SGs — standard SGs will not mount.
- Magne Flat Base requires Magnacore SG (North or South core).
- Support Parts (SAR) snap onto Neo SG only — not compatible with standard (non-Neo) SGs.

**Left-spin compatibility:**
- Left Spin Gear produces counter-clockwise rotation; most ARs perform identically either way.
- ARs labeled "L preferred" (Reverse Dragon, Reverse Wolf) have reverse-slope contact geometry that benefits from left spin; they are physically interchangeable with any SG but perform best with Left SG.
- Left Engine Gear types produce left-spin in EG bases.

**Engine Gear compatibility:**
- EG-specific bases: Engine Stopper Base, Full Auto Clutch Base, Gyro Engine Gear hosts.
- EG tips (Metal Flat, Metal Semi-Flat, Circle Defenser, Circle Survivor, Metal Ball, Turbo) cannot be swapped to standard Spin Gear bases.
- Left EGs fit only Left EG-compatible bases; Right EGs fit Right EG-compatible bases.
